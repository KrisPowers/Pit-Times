Do you want to work with Pit Times?

Pit Times, the self proclaimed largest Formula 1 Statistic and Highlights provider in the world has been community lead sense day one. Any one person can be part of our Board, also known as the team of people making our disissions. But it is a little more complex than that, for a person to join our Board, you must contribute finacly to the Services existent. This can come in the form of a Domain Mainainer which requires a small sum of money to be contributed to the purchasing of our PitTimes.com domain. You could also contribute by helping pay the salary of Pit Times engineers and brodcasters.

How do I contribute Funding?

For one to become a part of our Board, you must fund our services. To do so, you must contact our team via or email address which allows us to get in contact you and sort out a payment method. Each individual can purchase as many percentages (shares) as one could desire. A single share cost $5 (As of April 2022) and more depending on the amount purchased.

Responisbilites of a Share Holder

Anyone who holds shares to Pit Times will be added to a direct contact chat with all of the other holders where meetings, events, changes, & more will be decided. Meetings will take place normaly once every week, but that can change over time.
